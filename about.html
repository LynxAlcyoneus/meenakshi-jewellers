Our story

Three generations of craft

From a single bench in 1962 to a name families trust across India.

Meenakshi began as a small atelier where every necklace was drawn by hand before a gram of gold was ever cut. Six decades on, that discipline hasn't changed — only the number of families who carry our work to their most important days.

Every piece is BIS-hallmarked, weighed in front of you, and backed by a lifetime exchange guarantee. We believe trust is the only thing more precious than gold.
